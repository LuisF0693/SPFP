## Target Audience Segmentation

### Persona 1: The Solopreneur 🎯
**Profile:**
- Age: 25-40
- Income: R$5k-20k/month
- Status: Self-employed (freelancer, consultant, creator)
- Tech-savvy: Medium-High

**Pain Points:**
- Financial chaos (disorganized)
- No clear direction
- Tax complexity
- Fear of investing wrong
- Time poverty (can't spend hours planning)

**Desired Outcome:**
- Clarity on finances
- Peace of mind
- Autonomy (control own decisions)
- Time savings

**Key Message:**
> "Stop guessing. Get clarity. Take control."
> (Financial clarity in 5 minutes, not 5 hours)

**CTA Strategy:**
- Primary: "Começar com Plataforma" (Low friction, immediate value)
- Secondary: "Agendar Demo" (for hesitant/serious buyers)

## Conversion Funnel Architecture

```
AWARENESS (Hero Section)
├─ Headline: Transformation-focused
├─ Subheading: Emotional hook
└─ CTAs: Segment-appropriate

INTEREST (Features Section)
├─ 4 Key benefits (not features)
├─ Use customer outcomes
└─ Link to persona goals

CONSIDERATION (Social Proof)
├─ Testimonials (by segment if possible)
├─ Metrics/social proof
├─ Risk reversal (7-day guarantee)
└─ Build credibility

DECISION (Pricing Section)
├─ Clear value-to-cost mapping
├─ Feature comparison (benefits, not specs)
├─ Pricing psychology (highlight popular plan)
└─ Objection handling

ACTION (Forms/CTAs)
├─ Low-friction lead capture
├─ Clear next steps
├─ Mobile-optimized
└─ Confirmation messaging
```
