## FAQ

**Q: When should we launch?**
A: Within 7 days. Earlier is better to capture normal traffic patterns. Avoid major holidays or marketing campaigns.

**Q: How long does the test run?**
A: Minimum 14 days, ideally 21 days to account for weekly traffic variance.

**Q: Which variation should we expect to win?**
A: Variation B has 85% probability. It has the strongest psychological foundation and highest expected lift (+60%).

**Q: Can we stop early if we see a clear winner?**
A: Only if reaching p < 0.01 confidence (99%) and minimum 1,000 sample size. Don't stop at p < 0.05 after 1 week (false positive risk).

**Q: What if all three perform similarly?**
A: Declare Variation B as winner (recommended) based on highest lift potential. Or extend test by 1 week. Check for external factors affecting results.

**Q: What's the revenue impact?**
A: If Variation B wins (most likely): +$267,500/month at 10k hero visitors/month = +$3.21 million/year.

**Q: How do we prevent test manipulation?**
A: Keep real-time results private. Share results only after test completion. Set clear decision criteria upfront. Use statistical significance threshold.

**Q: What if the winner underperforms our baseline?**
A: This is unlikely given psychological backing. If all variants underperform, check for external factors (traffic quality, form issues, tracking errors).
