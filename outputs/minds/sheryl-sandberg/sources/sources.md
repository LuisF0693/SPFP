## Books (Primary Sources)

### 1. Lean In: Women, Work, and the Will to Lead (2013)
- **Author:** Sheryl Sandberg (com Nell Scovell)
- **Publisher:** Knopf
- **Reliability:** Primary — escrito em sua voz, experiências pessoais e pesquisa extensiva
- **Key themes:** Ownership na carreira, feedback bidirecional, sistemas organizacionais, liderança inclusiva, sentar à mesa
- **Notable quotes collected:**
  - "Done is better than perfect."
  - "What would you do if you weren't afraid?"
  - "Leadership is making others better as a result of your presence."
  - "Feedback is a gift — the people who care enough to give it are the ones worth keeping."
  - "In the future, there will be no female leaders. There will just be leaders."
  - "Igualdade real na liderança começa com igualdade real nos processos — não em intenções."
- **Coverage in clone:** Alta — frameworks de people management, feedback, ownership e cultura derivam principalmente deste livro

### 2. Option B: Facing Adversity, Building Resilience, and Finding Joy (2017)
- **Author:** Sheryl Sandberg e Adam Grant
- **Publisher:** Knopf
- **Reliability:** Primary — co-escrito com academico de referência, baseado em experiência pessoal e pesquisa científica
- **Key themes:** Resiliência como prática, os três Ps do pessimismo (permanence, pervasiveness, personalization), bounce forward, gestão de crise, post-mortem sem culpa
- **Notable quotes collected:**
  - "Option B is not what we wanted, but we can kick the shit out of Option B."
  - "Resilience is not a fixed trait. It's built. Cultivated. Practiced."
  - "We plant the seeds of resilience in how we process negative events."
  - "The ability to acknowledge and accept feelings helps us grow stronger."
- **Coverage in clone:** Moderada — informa o framework de resiliência organizacional e gestão de crises
