## Sobre Marketing e Permissão [src-001, src-008]

> "Permission marketing is the privilege (not the right) of delivering anticipated, personal and relevant messages to people who actually want to get them."
*— Permission Marketing, 1999*

> "Marketing is no longer about the stuff that you make, but about the stories you tell."
*— Purple Cow, 2003*

> "Marketing is the act of making change happen. Making is insufficient. You haven't made an impact until you've changed someone."
*— This Is Marketing, 2018*

> "The goal is not to do business with everybody who needs what you have. The goal is to do business with people who believe what you believe."
*— This Is Marketing, 2018*

> "The smallest viable audience: If you don't know who you're making something for, how can you make it better?"
*— This Is Marketing, 2018*

> "People like us do things like this. That's it. That's the whole game."
*— This Is Marketing, 2018*

## Sobre The Dip — Quando Desistir [src-003]

> "Winners quit all the time. They just quit the right stuff at the right time."
*— The Dip, 2007*

> "The Dip is the long stretch between beginner's luck and real accomplishment. The Dip is the combination of bureaucracy and busywork you must deal with in order to get to the next level."
*— The Dip, 2007*

> "The question is not whether you'll face a Dip. The question is: what are you going to do about it?"
*— The Dip, 2007*

> "Cul-de-sacs (French for dead end) and Dips don't feel the same. The Cul-de-sac is a situation where you work and you work and you work and nothing much changes."
*— The Dip, 2007*

## Sobre Tribos e Liderança [src-004, src-015]

> "A tribe is a group of people connected to one another, connected to a leader, and connected to an idea."
*— Tribes, 2008*

> "Leadership is the art of giving people a platform for spreading ideas that work."
*— Tribes, 2008*

> "Heretics are the new leaders. The ones who challenge the status quo, who get out in front of their tribes, who create movements."
*— Tribes, 2008*

> "Management is about manipulating resources to get a known job done. Leadership is about creating change that you believe in."
*— Tribes, 2008*

> "The secret of leadership is simple: Do what you believe in. Paint a picture of the future. Go there. People will follow."
*— Tribes, 2008*
