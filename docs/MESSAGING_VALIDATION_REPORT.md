## 5. RECOMMENDATIONS & REFINEMENTS

### 5.1 Data-Driven Messaging Adjustments

**Based on validation analysis, recommend these refinements:**

#### Refinement 1: Strengthen Transformation Framework Subheading

**Current:** "Em apenas 5 minutos, com IA que entende você"

**Enhanced Option:** "Em apenas 5 minutos, com IA que aprende com você"

**Rationale:** "Aprende com você" vs "entende você" emphasizes personalization + user control more powerfully. "Aprende" = continuous adaptation, building stronger relationship signal.

---

#### Refinement 2: Reposition Social Proof for Maximum Impact

**Current:** Social proof appears after pricing (Test 4 proposes before pricing)

**Recommendation:** Place testimonials BEFORE pricing section with specific quote:

```
"10k+ brasileiros já fizeram sua transformação financeira aqui"
[Carousel of 3-4 testimonials emphasizing before/after clarity story]
```

**Rationale:** Trust-before-ask converts 20-30% higher. Testimonials positioned before pricing overcome last-minute objections.

---

#### Refinement 3: Add Specificity to "10k+ Users" Social Proof

**Current:** "10k+ users confide daily"

**Enhanced Option:** "10k+ brasileiros (média: R$20k/mês) organizam suas finanças com SPFP"

**Rationale:** Adding income range makes social proof more relatable. Users want proof from people "like them."
